About YX-LASER

Taizhou Yuxuan Photoelectric Technology Co., Ltd.

We are a professional laser equipment manufacturer located in Taizhou, Zhejiang, China. With over 10 years of industry experience, we focus on R&D, production and export of high-quality laser machines.


Our factory covers more than 3,000 square meters, equipped with standardized production lines, advanced testing equipment and experienced engineers. We strictly control every production process to ensure stable performance and reliable quality for each machine.


Our main products include CO₂ laser cutters, fiber laser cutters, laser marking machines, laser welding machines, laser cleaning machines and vibrating knife cutters. These machines are widely used in signage, textile, metalworking, packaging, automotive and other industries.


We have exported our products to more than 60 countries and regions around the world. We are committed to providing customers with cost-effective laser solutions and professional after-sales service.
